Join the journey

If you are interested in joining the YES program and becoming a true leader , we greatly encourage you to apply. Not just in Mali, The YES program is open to high school students from around the world who are interested in cultural exchange and leadership development. You will be amazed at how much you will learn and grow during your time in the United States. The YES program is a life-changing experience that will help you develop your leadership skills, build lifelong friendships, and gain a deeper understanding of different cultures.
To learn more about the application process and eligibility requirements, please visit the official YES program website at YES Program
We look forward to welcoming you to the YES family!
