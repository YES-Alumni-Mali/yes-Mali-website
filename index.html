YES Program

The Youth Exchange and Study program, or YES program, is a program funded by the U.S. Department of State. As its name suggests, the YES program gives youth leaders and talented individuals the opportunity to explore the United States, share their cultures, and help others understand them. The goal of this website is to help you understand the impact of this program through the lens of those who have participated in it.

source:Kennedy Lugar YES-program

Hello! Welcome to the Kennedy Lugar YES program: MALI. We are young Malians and we have been sharing our cultures and exploring the United States for a year! Now as YES alumni, it is our pleasure to share our experiences, challenges, and triumphs with you. Join us on this journey and discover the beauty of cultural exchange and the power of youth leadership.
