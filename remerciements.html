II-To our Organizations:
  • I-Earn Mali:

I-Earn Mali is a non-profit organization that promotes cultural exchange and education. Since 2007, they have impacted the lives of more than 175 Malian students by helping them participate in the Kennedy-Lugar Youth Exchange and Study Program. They work in Partnership with the US Embassy in Mali. Today, the office is 18 years old, and we take this opportunity to thank our uncles and aunties who work hard every day to make our dreams come true. Thanks, I-Earn Mali.

    As you have probably seen on the websites, placement organizations are those that help us during our exchange year in the US. They are the link between our country and American host families. They find us local coordinators to help us better navigate our new environment, guide us, and give us all the resources we need. No matter the state, placement organizations have a huge impact on the success of our exchange year. A big thank you to all the people working within those organizations.

Finally, we want to say thank you to all the American people who have welcomed us into their homes, the US Department of State, and the US Embassy in Mali for giving us the ultimate chance to create something greater than ourselves.
Thank you.
